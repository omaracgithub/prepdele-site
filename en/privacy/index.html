Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

DELE B1 Practice ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Account data (if you sign up): email address and name. Required to sync your progress across devices.
  • Practice progress: answers to questions, scores, times. Stored locally and, if signed in, on our server.
  • Anonymous analytics: screens visited, usage events. These do not personally identify you.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not record audio during speaking practice. Speaking tasks are for self-assessment only.
  • We do not sell data to third parties.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple App Store — distribution and purchases.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time from the app (Account → Delete Account) or by writing to hello@prepdele.com.

Contact

For any privacy questions: hello@prepdele.com.
