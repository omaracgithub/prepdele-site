Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Prep DELE (we) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Account data (if you sign up): email and name. Required to sync your progress across devices.
  • Practice progress: question responses, scores, times. Stored locally and, if signed in, on our server.
  • Anonymous analytics: screens visited, usage events. These do not personally identify you.

Voice recordings

We may listen to recordings of conversations for quality improvement purposes. These recordings are used exclusively to improve the accuracy and experience of the app. They are not shared with third parties.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not sell data to third parties.

Third-party services

We use the following services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple App Store — distribution and purchases.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time from the app (Account → Delete account) or by emailing hello@prepdele.com.

Contact

For any privacy questions: hello@prepdele.com.
